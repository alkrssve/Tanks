You can have up to 200HP at one time, each gravity flip costs 5HP with a maximum of five flips while in the air.





No matter the color of the opponent tank you will take 25HP when hit by their bullets.







Standard pips provide 1 Ammo, shiny pips provide 25HP, and square pips provide a power up dependent on color ( White = Ammo Pack, Blue = Bullet Effect, Purple = Shield/Health, Yellow = Special).



Overtime the arena will start to fall apart creating holes your tank could fall into, after 3 minutes you will lose 2HP per second.
Move left and right using arrow key or A and D.







Flip gravity up using the up or W key, flip gravity down using the down or S key.







Aim with mouse click to shoot.
